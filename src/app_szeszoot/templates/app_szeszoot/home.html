Welcome to Szeszoot!

Known issues with Safari! If you want to avoid them please use some other browser (we recommend any chromium-based one).

We're gonna fix it sooner or later!

If you have any remarks please open an issue on project's github page.

Usage: 1) As a Game Master click "Set up game" (you must be logged in) 2) Then choose one of available quizzes 3) On the Game Master Panel that appears, there is game id. Share it with players you want to join the game. 4) Wait for the players to join (their nicknames will appear on the Game Master Panel) 5) As a Player, click "Play!" (you don't have to be logged in) 6) Then type your nickname of choice and game id you want to join 7) Click "Join" 8) As a Game Master click "Start!" once all players have joined the game 9) Click "Next Question!" when you want to push next question to Players 10) Rest of steps is quite intuitive :) HAVE FUN!
